Why Kobayashi is Best Girl
Posted on: November 1, 2023

Let me explain why Kobayashi is the best character, even if I don't particularly like the show she's from.

She's just a normal office worker who deals with all this crazy dragon stuff in such a relatable way. Plus her deadpan expressions are golden.

Not to mention she's basically the only sane person in the entire series.
